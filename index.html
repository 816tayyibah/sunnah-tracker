🍽️ Eating Duas

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ وَعَلٰى بَرَكَةِ اللّٰهِ
Bismillahi wa 'ala barakatillah
In the name of Allah and with the blessings of Allah
🍽️ After Eating — کھانے کے بعد
اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِيْ أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِيْنَ
Alhamdulillahillazi at'amana wa saqana wa ja'alana minal muslimeen
Praise be to Allah who fed us, gave us drink, and made us Muslims
🚽 Washroom Duas
🚽 Before Entering — داخل ہونے سے پہلے
اَللّٰهُمَّ إِنِّيْ أَعُوْذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْخُبُثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ
Allahumma inni a'oozu bika minal khubuthi wal khabaa'ith
O Allah, I seek Your protection from evil male and female jinns
🚽 After Leaving — نکلنے کے بعد

🏠 Home & Traveling Duas
🏠 Leaving Home — گھر سے نکلتے وقت
بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللّٰهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللّٰهِ
Bismillahi tawakkaltu 'alallah, la hawla wala quwwata illa billah
In the name of Allah, I trust in Allah. There is no power except with Allah
🏠 Entering Home — گھر میں داخل ہوتے وقت
اَللّٰهُمَّ إِنِّيْ أَسْأَلُكَ خَيْرَ الْمَوْلَجِ وَخَيْرَ الْمَخْرَجِ
Allahumma inni as'aluka khairal mawliji wa khairal makhraji
O Allah, I ask You for the best of entering and the best of leaving
🚗 Before Riding / Traveling — سواری سے پہلے
سُبْحَانَ الَّذِيْ سَخَّرَ لَنَا هٰذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِيْنَ
Subhanallazi sakhkhara lana haza wa ma kunna lahu muqrineen
Glory to Him who has subjected this to us, for we could never have done it ourselves
💧 Wudu Duas
💧 Before Wudu — وضو سے پہلے
بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ
Bismillah
In the name of Allah
💧 After Wudu & Bath — 1️⃣ Kalima Shahadat
أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَّا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللّٰهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُوْلُهُ
Ashhadu alla ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lahu wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan 'abduhu wa rasuluh
I bear witness that there is no god but Allah alone, and that Muhammad ﷺ is His servant and messenger
💧 After Wudu & Bath — 2️⃣ Allahumma Inni
اَللّٰهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِيْ مِنَ التَّوَّابِيْنَ وَاجْعَلْنِيْ مِنَ الْمُتَطَهِّرِيْنَ
Allahumma ij'alni minat tawwabeena waj'alni minal mutatahhireen
O Allah, make me among those who repent and among those who purify themselves
💧 After Wudu & Bath — 3️⃣ Subhanaka Allahumma
سُبْحَانَكَ اللّٰهُمَّ وَبِحَمْدِكَ أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَّا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ أَسْتَغْفِرُكَ وَأَتُوْبُ إِلَيْكَ
Subhanakallahumma wa bihamdika ashhadu alla ilaha illa anta astaghfiruka wa atoobu ilayk
Glory be to You O Allah, I bear witness there is no god but You, I seek Your forgiveness and repent to You
😴 Sleeping & Waking Up Duas
😴 Before Sleeping — سونے سے پہلے
اَللّٰهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوْتُ وَأَحْيَا
Allahumma bismika amootu wa ahya
O Allah, in Your name I die and I live
😴 Before Sleeping — کلمہ شہادت
أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَّا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا اللّٰهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيْكَ لَهُ وَأَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُوْلُهُ
Ashhadu alla ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lahu wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan 'abduhu wa rasuluh
I bear witness there is no god but Allah alone, and Muhammad ﷺ is His servant and messenger
😴 Before Sleeping — سورۃ الاخلاص
قُلْ هُوَ اللّٰهُ أَحَدٌ ۚ اَللّٰهُ الصَّمَدُ ۚ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُوْلَدْ ۚ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَّهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ
Qul huwallahu ahad. Allahus samad. Lam yalid walam yoolad. Walam yakun lahu kufuwan ahad.
Say: He is Allah, the One. Allah, the Eternal Refuge. He neither begets nor is born. Nor is there any equal to Him
😴 Before Sleeping — سورۃ الفلق
قُلْ أَعُوْذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ ۚ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ۚ وَمِنْ شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ ۚ وَمِنْ شَرِّ النَّفَّاثَاتِ فِي الْعُقَدِ ۚ وَمِنْ شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ
Qul a'oozu bi rabbil falaq. Min sharri ma khalaq. Wa min sharri ghasiqin iza waqab. Wa min sharrin naffasati fil 'uqad. Wa min sharri hasidin iza hasad.
Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of the daybreak, from the evil of what He created, and from the evil of darkness when it settles, and from the evil of those who blow on knots, and from the evil of an envier when he envies
😴 Before Sleeping — سورۃ الناس
قُلْ أَعُوْذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ ۚ مَلِكِ النَّاسِ ۚ إِلٰهِ النَّاسِ ۚ مِنْ شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ ۚ الَّذِيْ يُوَسْوِسُ فِيْ صُدُوْرِ النَّاسِ ۚ مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ
Qul a'oozu bi rabbin nas. Malikin nas. Ilahin nas. Min sharril waswaasil khannas. Allazi yuwaswisu fi sudoorin nas. Minal jinnati wan nas.
Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind, the King of mankind, the God of mankind, from the evil of the retreating whisperer, who whispers in the hearts of mankind, from among the jinn and mankind
☀️ After Waking Up — جاگنے کے بعد
اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِيْ أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُوْرُ
Alhamdulillahillazi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilaihin nushoor
Praise be to Allah who gave us life after death, and to Him is the resurrection
